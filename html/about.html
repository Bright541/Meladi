Meladi is a song-making and song-listening app that allows users to compose their own songs, raps, or even phonk vocals without any need for money or equipment. It empowers creativity by providing an easy-to-use platform for anyone to express themselves musically.

Features

- Create music easily without any professional equipment.
- Discover a variety of beats and sounds to enhance your compositions.
- Share your creations with friends or the public.

How It Works

Once you sign up, you can start composing immediately using our intuitive interface. Add your lyrics, choose beats, and record your vocals—all in one place.

The best part? You don’t need any fancy equipment or paywall restrictions to start making music. While there is a premium version available, the core features are accessible to everyone, enabling anyone to dive into music-making without barriers.
